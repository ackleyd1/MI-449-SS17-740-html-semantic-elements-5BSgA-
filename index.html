Mom's Spaghetti

  • 1 package of thin spaghetti
  • 1 package of italian sausage
  • 2 lbs of ground beef
  • 2-28oz cans Starcross tomatoe sauce
  • 1-6oz can of tomatoe paste
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • pinch of basil
  1. Brown the ground beef in a large saucepot
  2. Drain the grease
  3. Add the tomatoe sauce, tomatoe paste, sugar, salt, baking soda, and garlic
  4. Simmer on low for 2 hours
  5. After 30 minutes, cook the italian sausage and add in around the 1hr mark
  6. With 30 minutes left, bring a pot of water to boil for the noodles and add the basil to the sauce
  7. Cook the noodles until desired softness
  8. The sauce and the noodles should finish around the same time. Enjoy!

Cheesy Potatoes

  • 2lbs frozen diced potatoes, thawed
  • 1 cup of diced onions
  • 2 cans of cream and chicken soup
  • 1- 16oz tub of sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted
  • 1- 8oz package of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Thaw the frozen diced potatoes
  2. Dice 1 cup of onions
  3. Mix ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Spread out evenly in a 9 x 13 inch greased baking pan
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour
Rating: 3/5 professional calvinball players

The original recipe called for a whole stick of butter but David Ackley has proved it to taste just as good using only 2 tablespoons of butter while also cutting back on the fat content.
